## Caption (双语)

[EN]

Before there was Fire or Water, there was simply Yin and Yang.

It is the oldest and most foundational concept in Chinese cosmology — the idea that everything in the universe arises from the dance of two opposite but complementary forces.

Yin and Yang are NOT good versus evil. They are not a moral battle. They are two energies that create, sustain, and transform each other.

**YIN** is receptive, dark, cool, still, inward, lunar, feminine in its energy.
**YANG** is active, bright, warm, moving, outward, solar, masculine in its energy.

Day needs night to exist. Summer needs winter. Inhale needs exhale. One cannot be defined without the other.

In BaZi (Chinese astrology), this duality determines the fundamental nature of your birth chart. Is your energy naturally outgoing (Yang) or introspective (Yin)? Is your expression expressive or reserved? Are you a fire that blazes outward or water that flows inward?

Every pillar in your chart carries a Yin or Yang label on one of the Five Elements. This is where the 10 Heavenly Stems come from — the Yin and Yang versions of Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water.

Your chart's Yin/Yang balance shapes:
- Whether you express emotions openly or keep them close
- Whether you take initiative or respond to what comes
- Whether your natural energy expands outward or recharges inward

There is no "better" energy — only the energy that is yours.

Which force moves through you?
