## Caption (双语)

[EN]

Does your love life mirror your career — or clash with it?

In BaZi (Chinese astrology), the answer is written in your dominant element. Each of the Five Elements shapes how you love and how you lead. Today, we explore FIRE — the element of passion, charisma, and transformation.

If Fire runs strong in your chart, here is what it means:

IN LOVE:
You love like a flame — intensely, generously, without reservation. You are drawn to partners who challenge and inspire you. Your warmth attracts people naturally, but you need someone who can match your energy without being consumed. You give your whole heart, yet you also need space to burn bright on your own terms.

IN CAREER:
You lead not by title but by presence. People follow you because you make things happen. You thrive in roles that let you inspire, innovate, and transform. Routine drains you. You need visible impact — projects where you can see the fire you lit spreading. Your superpower is turning vision into momentum. Your challenge is pacing yourself so you do not burn out before the finish line.

But Fire is only one of five. Does your chart burn with Fire, flow with Water, stand with Earth, cut with Metal, or grow with Wood?

Every birth chart is a unique constellation of these elements. One always dominates — and it shapes everything about how you connect and create.

Which element are you in love and career?

DM us for a personal BaZi reading. We will reveal your dominant element and how to harness it in relationships and work.
