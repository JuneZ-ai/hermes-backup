### Key Finding
The analysis notes captured the "理" (说得着/说不着 framework, 绕的结构, 人物链) but missed the "味" — specifically:

**The novel's deepest truth is not in what is spoken, but in what is never spoken.** 吴摩西 never tells 巧玲 his final words. 章楚红 never tells 牛爱国 her final words. 杨百顺 never speaks one sentence with 罗长礼. The book's title "一句顶一万句" refers to a sentence that never gets said.

This connects to the user's 以术载道 framework: technique (speaking well) serves the relationship (道), but the most important communication is not about technique at all — it is about presence, trust, and the space between people.
